Setup Your Account

Create or Activate Your UHC Account

  • Visit activate.uhc.com and click "Create Account".
  • Enter your name, DOB, and member ID.
  • Set a secure password and verify your identity.
  • Complete email verification.
  • Login to access your personalized dashboard.
Forgot Password?

Reset Your UHC Password

  • Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password".
  • Enter your registered email or member ID.
  • Follow the link sent to your email.
  • Create a new secure password.
