Setup Your Account

Create or Activate Your UHC Account

  • Go to activate.uhc.com and click "Create Account".
  • Enter your personal details including full name, date of birth, and member ID.
  • Set a strong password and complete security questions.
  • Verify your email address to activate the account.
  • Log in to your dashboard to access features.
  • Update your profile for a personalized healthcare experience.
  • Enable notifications to stay informed on claims, appointments, and benefits.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use a strong, unique password and update it regularly.
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available.
  • Log out after each session, especially on shared devices.
  • Keep your browser and antivirus updated for security.
  • Verify emails and links to avoid phishing scams.
  • Check your account activity periodically for any unusual behavior.

Reset Your UHC Password

  • Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page.
  • Enter your registered email or member ID.
  • Follow the instructions sent via email.
  • Create a new secure password to login.
  • Update your security questions for extra protection.
