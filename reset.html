Reset Your UHC Account Password

Follow our step-by-step guide to securely reset your UHC password and regain access to your healthcare account online.

Reset Now

Introduction

Forgetting your UHC account password is common. This guide helps you reset it safely, regain access, and protect your healthcare information online.

Step-by-Step Password Reset Process

Step 1: Go to Login Page

Open your browser and navigate to activate.uhc.com. Click on "Sign In".

Step 2: Click "Forgot Password?"

Below the login form, click the "Forgot Password?" link to start the reset process.

Step 3: Enter Email or Member ID

Provide your registered email address or UHC member ID and complete any verification prompts.

Step 4: Check Email

Open the email sent by UHC with the password reset link and instructions.

Step 5: Create New Password

Choose a strong password combining letters, numbers, and symbols, then confirm it.

Step 6: Login

Return to the login page and access your account using the new password.

Tips for Strong Passwords

  • Use a unique, strong password.
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available.
  • Never share your credentials.
  • Update passwords regularly.
  • Always log out from shared devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't get the reset email?

Check your spam folder or verify the email you entered is correct.

Can I reset multiple times?

Yes, but links expire quickly. Request a new one if necessary.

What if my account is locked?

Wait a few minutes or contact UHC support for help.
